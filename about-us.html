About Us

LBS is one of the leading institutions in the city of Biratnagar run by the energetic team of academic professionals since last 17 years. We run from Toddles, Kindergarten level to grade 12. We have separate blocks for Montessori (Toddlers to U.KG), grade one to 10 and completely separate blocks for grade 11 and 12. We provide the following facilities:

  • Transport facility for students.
  • Hostel facility for students.
  • Spacious and airy class rooms with audio visual facilities.
  • Well-equipped science and computer lab facility.
  • Attractive library with enough books.
  • Day Boarding system with the facilities of lunch, tiffin, games, etc.
